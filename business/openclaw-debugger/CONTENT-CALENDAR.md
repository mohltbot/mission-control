## Content Formats That Work

### Twitter/X
- **Threads:** 5-10 tweets, educational
- **Quick tips:** Single tweet, actionable
- **Polls:** Engage community, gather intel
- **Case studies:** Story format
- **Recaps:** Build in public

### IndieHackers
- **Launch posts:** "I built X"
- **Case studies:** Detailed breakdowns
- **Lessons learned:** Reflection posts
- **AMA:** "Ask me about OpenClaw"

### LinkedIn
- **Professional angles:** Business impact
- **Stories:** Client wins, lessons
- **Industry takes:** AI automation trends

### Discord
- **Helpful replies:** Answer questions
- **Value adds:** Share tips/tools
- **Soft mentions:** "DM if stuck"

## Content Creation Tips

### Batch Creation
- Write 5 tweets in one session
- Schedule with TweetDeck or similar
- Repurpose across platforms

### Templates
- Keep 5 thread templates ready
- Reuse hooks that worked
- Standardize case study format

### Engagement Rules
- Reply to every comment in first hour
- Quote tweet when someone mentions you
- Thank people who share your content

### Timing
- Twitter: 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM PST
- LinkedIn: 8 AM, 12 PM PST
- IndieHackers: Any time, post on weekdays
