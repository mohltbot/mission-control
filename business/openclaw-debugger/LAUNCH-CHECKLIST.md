## If Nothing's Working by Day 3

### Pivot Options:
1. **Lower price further** — $15 first fix just for reviews
2. **Change channel** — If Twitter isn't working, blitz LinkedIn
3. **Broaden offer** — "AI agent debugging" (not just OpenClaw)
4. **Find partners** — Reach out to AI agencies, offer rev share

### Debug Your Launch:
- Are you messaging the right people?
- Is your offer clear?
- Are you adding value first?
- Is your price too high for unknown brand?
