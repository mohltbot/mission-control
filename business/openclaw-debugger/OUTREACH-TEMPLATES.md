```

```

- Your time debugging: 3-4 hours
- Your hourly rate: $X
- Cost to DIY: $[3-4x hourly rate]

My fix: $75, done in 30 minutes.

Plus you learn what went wrong so you can fix it yourself next time.

```
Full refund, no questions asked. 

I only take gigs I'm confident I can solve. If I can't, you don't pay.
```

## Conversion Optimization

### Reduce Friction:
- Offer to look at the error first (free)
- Give quick DIY tip in initial response
- Make payment easy (Fiverr handles this)
- Promise fast turnaround

### Increase Urgency:
- "I have 2 slots this week"
- "Most fixes done same day"
- "Your agents are down — let's get them back up"

### Build Trust:
- Share anonymized case studies
- Offer money-back guarantee
- Show screenshots of successful fixes (with permission)
