## Quick Tips for Sending

1. **Personalize the first line** - Reference something specific from their profile
2. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am** in their timezone
3. **Wait 5-7 days** between messages
4. **Stop after 2 follow-ups** - don't be annoying
5. **Track in a simple spreadsheet** - name, company, date sent, response
