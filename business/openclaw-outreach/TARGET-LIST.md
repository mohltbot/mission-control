# Target List: Where to Find Older Business Owners

> Specific places to find your ideal prospects

---

## LinkedIn Search Queries

### Boolean Search Strings

**Architecture Firms:**
```
("owner" OR "founder" OR "principal") AND ("architecture" OR "architectural") AND ("firm" OR "studio")
```

**Manufacturing:**
```
("owner" OR "founder" OR "president") AND ("manufacturing" OR "fabrication" OR "production") AND ("company" OR "inc" OR "llc")
```

**Professional Services:**
```
("owner" OR "partner" OR "founding partner") AND ("CPA" OR "accountant" OR "attorney" OR "lawyer" OR "law firm")
```

**General SMB Owners 50+:**
```
("founder" OR "owner" OR "CEO") AND ("small business" OR "family owned") NOT ("seeking" OR "looking for")
```

### Filters to Apply

- **Location:** Your target geography
- **Company size:** 2-50 employees
- **Industry:** Select relevant categories
- **Connections:** 2nd degree (warm intros possible)
- **Keywords:** "founded" + year (look for 1990s-2000s = older owners)

### Profile Signals to Look For

- Profile photo looks 50+
- Company founded 15+ years ago
- No "automation" or "AI" in headline
- Posts about "busy" or "overwhelmed"
- Manual processes mentioned in experience

## Warm Intro Opportunities

### Existing Connections to Tap

**Ask your network:**
- "Do you know any architecture firm owners who seem overwhelmed by admin work?"
- "Who's the oldest business owner you know who's struggling with technology?"
- "Any manufacturing owners in your network who complain about paperwork?"

**Look for connectors:**
- Business bankers
- Commercial real estate agents
- Business insurance agents
- CPAs who serve multiple businesses
- Business attorneys
- Commercial lenders

### Creating Warm Intros

**Template for asking your network:**
```
Hey [Name],

I'm helping small business owners automate repetitive tasks and save time. Do you know any [industry] owners who are 50+ and might be drowning in admin work?

Not looking for a hard sell—just a warm intro to someone who might find this helpful.

Thanks!
[Your Name]
```

### Strategic Partnerships

**Potential referral partners:**
- IT consultants (they see the pain, don't solve it)
- Business coaches (clients need implementation)
- Bookkeepers (see the manual processes)
- Executive assistants (know what wastes time)

**Offer:** 10-20% referral fee or reciprocal referrals

## Daily Prospecting Routine (30 min/day)

1. **LinkedIn search** (10 min) - Run 1 search, find 5 prospects
2. **Personalize and send** (10 min) - Send 3 connection requests with notes
3. **Follow-ups** (5 min) - Check for responses, send follow-ups to warm leads
4. **Group engagement** (5 min) - Comment on 2 posts in industry groups

**Weekly:**
- Review and update target list
- Attend 1 virtual or in-person event
- Ask 1 person in your network for warm intros
